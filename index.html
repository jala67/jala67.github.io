VR Game: SpellWeaver

Spellweaver: Words of Power is a group project that delves into the realm of Speech-to-Text technology in Virtual Reality. In this captivating Unity game, you step into the shoes of a magician, wielding the power of words. Your task is to accurately recite spells from a spellbook. The correctness of your incantation directly influences the potency of your unleashed spell, determined by the Word Error Rate.

Our project underwent rigorous testing of various STT APIs, ensuring not only seamless integration but also future adaptability through the implementation of the Adapter pattern.
